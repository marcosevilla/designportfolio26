## Artist bio

Marco Sevilla (he/him) is a queer Filipino photographer and designer born and raised in the Bay Area, currently based in San Francisco. He's built a body of work documenting locally organized queer nightlife events, such as Queen Out, and various concerts for Live Nation and Goldenvoice. 

He aims to transport the viewer onto the dance floor by capturing intimate moments of catharsis and celebration. With his work, he hopes to honor the artists and organizers that shape SF nightlife, and continue to create space for queer liberation.
